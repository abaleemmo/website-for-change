How can YOU help?

Donate

Support the United Nations Relief and Works Agency (UNRWA) in their mission to provide support and resources to Palestinian refugees.


Fund HEAL Palestine's efforts to bring proper healthcare and humanitarian aid to Palestinians.


Aid Oxfam in providing Gazans with adequate food and clean water.

Educate

Read about the history of the Israel-Palestine conflict from Al Jazeera.


Search through Decolonize Palestine's myths database.


Browse through Teach Palestine's catalog of teaching materials aimed at different age groups.

Petition

Join Amnesty International's petition for a ceasefire by all parties in Gaza.


Contact your congressional representatives to call for a ceasefire.


Sign Action Network's petition for the United States to work towards allowing humanitarian aid into Gaza.

Stay informed and up to date on the situation in Gaza and keep an eye out for any other resources or ways to help.
